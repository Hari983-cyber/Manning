Our Hiring Process

With 18+ years of experience in BPO bulk hiring, leadership recruitment, and IT talent acquisition, we deliver a fast, structured, and quality-driven hiring process tailored to your growth goals.

01
Discovery & Role Scoping
  • Understand your business goals, team structure, and success metrics
  • Define role requirements, competencies, and cultural alignment
  • Finalize timelines, SLAs, and communication cadence
02
Talent Mapping & Sourcing
  • Map active and passive talent across relevant markets
  • Multi-channel sourcing via databases, referrals, and communities
  • Custom pipelines for volume, niche, and leadership roles
03
Screening & Assessment
  • Structured screening aligned to role scorecards
  • BPO: aptitude, communication, and shift-readiness checks
  • IT & Leadership: technical and competency-based evaluations
04
Shortlist & Client Review
  • Curated shortlist with detailed candidate insights
  • Diversity-conscious candidate slates
  • Transparent reporting and candidate comparison
05
Interviews & Coordination
  • End-to-end interview scheduling and candidate preparation
  • Panel alignment with structured interview kits
  • Continuous feedback loops for faster alignment
06
Offer Management & Negotiation
  • Compensation benchmarking and expectation alignment
  • Offer rollout, notice-period handling, and counter-offer mitigation
  • Pre-joining engagement to improve joining ratios
07
Onboarding & Post-Placement Support
  • Background verification and documentation support
  • 30–90 day check-ins with clients and candidates
  • Replacement guarantee as per agreed terms

What Sets Us Apart

Deep domain expertise across BPO bulk hiring, leadership, and IT roles, delivered with speed, compliance, and data-driven insights — ensuring measurable hiring outcomes and long-term value.
