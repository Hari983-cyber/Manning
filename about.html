Manning Consulting: Executive Search Firm

Founded in 2008 in New Delhi, Manning Consulting is a leading executive search and talent consulting firm specializing in senior management and leadership hiring across high-impact domains. With a proactive and relationship-driven approach, we deliver strategic talent solutions that help organizations build strong, future-ready leadership

Over the past 18+ years, we have partnered with 100+ multinational and mid-market enterprises, guided by our belief in connecting the right talent with the right opportunity. We consistently bridge organizational needs with individual career aspirations while maintaining the highest standards of quality, speed, and execution.

Our journey reflects continuous evolution—from early success in ITES and BPO hiring to diversified expertise across consulting, BFSI, digital operations, analytics, automation, robotics, machine learning, and GenAI. This adaptability enables us to address complex and niche leadership requirements across industries.

With a global presence in New Delhi and London and a team of 60+ experienced HR consultants, we support Fortune 100 and growth-focused organizations across India, the UK, USA, Singapore, UAE, and Saudi Arabia. Our domain-specialist teams deliver customized talent solutions across 15+ industry practices worldwide.
